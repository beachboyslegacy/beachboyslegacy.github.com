    Recorded live in Santa Barbara CA, March 2000

Jeff Barry, one of the most successful popular songwriters of the century, invited some of his favorite performers to star in a special concert taped in Santa Barbara, California. The show features many classic songs sung by some of our favorite performers of all-time including Brian Wilson singing 'Be My Baby', Mary Wilson of The Supremes belting out 'River Deep Mountain High', and Ronnie Spector giving a special charge to 'I Can Hear Music'. Also featured are The Dixie Cups, The Crystals, Andy Kim and many others. The artists and Jeff also talk about the songs, giving new insight into the writing and production of these classic songs. 'Chapel of Love' captures these special moments in a once-in-a-lifetime event.
