  • There is no more perfect window into Brian's raw soul, or his musical/personal world, than this lovely record. See past the rasping, forced, tar-ravaged vocals, into the delicious chords; buy into the sheer innocence of the naive rhymes and the inspired, sparce production. A joy.

    Sean Macreavy
