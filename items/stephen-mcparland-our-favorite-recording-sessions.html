    94 Beach Boys and related recording sessions circa 1961-70, based on official A.F.M. (American Federation of Musicians) recording contracts, all of which are reproduced. In addition, the book also dissects and reconstructs the events (based on interviews with those involved) circa Summer 1961-Summer 1962 that saw the birth of The Beach Boys as a group.
