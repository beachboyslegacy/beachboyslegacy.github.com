    “Come on along as I take you on a thirty-year safari of Beach Boys concert experiences. What started out as a lark has turned into a lifelong passion and the memories – both good and bad – have been documented in this book. The text reveals timely information such as ever-changing band line ups, setlists and even my personal life. Original photos accompany the text and capture many of the memorable moments as they occurred. If you are a fan of The Beach Boys you should enjoy reading this book. Perhaps you can relate to the experiences that are revealed, with no details left behind. Well, except for maybe a few!” Surf’s Up, Andrew L. Lewand
